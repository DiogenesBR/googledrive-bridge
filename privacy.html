Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This application serves as a client-side bridge to facilitate authentication with Google Drive. By using this service, you acknowledge the following:

1. Data Collection & Usage

This application is purely client-side. It does not collect, store, or share your personal data or files on any external server managed by the developer.

2. Google Drive Access

We request access to your Google Drive strictly to:

  • Authenticate your identity via OAuth 2.0.
  • Read and write specific files created by the application (using the drive.file scope).

3. Third-Party Sharing

Your data is transmitted directly between your browser and Google's servers. No information is shared with third parties.

4. Contact

For inquiries, please refer to the contact information provided on the GitHub profile hosting this repository.
